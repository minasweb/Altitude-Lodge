about us

Welcmoe to the Lodge! Altitude Lodge offers a completely off-grid experience for nature lovers. Our A-frame cabins are constructed from local Astralian timbers, set within the breathtaking Blue Monthains.

Relax with a cup of coffee on the deck and hear the sounds of the local wildlife. At night, warm up around a crackling fire under the Southers Cross.

If hikind is your thing, visit some of the breathtaking lookouts and stunning waterfalls the area has to offer. There are plenty of natural waterfalls and trails to explore.
